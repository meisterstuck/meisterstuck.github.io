You can modify the request parameters of the url, available options:

  • 1. language:
    lang

    1. en (default)
    2. cn
    3. hk

  • 2. suite numbers(tracking objects number):
    room

    1. 1
    2. 2 (default)
    3. 3
    4. 4
    5. 5

  • 2. hotel property names:
    property

    1. parisianmacao (default)
    2. venetianmacao
    3. holidayinn
    4. conrad
    5. sandsmacao

Example entrance:

  • > Default
  • > Simple chinese, 4 rooms, Parisian Macao
  • > Traditional chinese, 5 rooms, Venetian Macao
